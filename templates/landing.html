Getting Started

1. Access The Library

Your local workspace Library safely caches and tracks every process diagram you open or edit. Access them offline quickly or spin up fresh blank templates directly from the prompt.

2. Advanced Editor

Draw complex automation logic. Add dynamic metadata to nodes, link pools to lanes, and auto-sync changes directly to the enterprise core safely.

3. Embed & Collaborate

When you finish authoring, send direct view-only links to stakeholders. Lock features prevent accidental collision editing from multiple authors at once.

Best Practices

Always name your Process metadata adequately (Project Name, Description) so the architecture team can quickly discern your diagram's purpose inside the overarching backend schema. Do not keep unused tabs active on the editor, as your session will hold a physical lock preventing co-workers from making adjustments.

Automated Deletions

Process diagrams deleted via the internal Library will only perform a Soft-Delete. To fully drop data or restore structures, please contact your systems administrator or write to {{ CONTACT_EMAIL }}.
